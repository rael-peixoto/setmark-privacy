Library

Workout

Built for the middle of a set

Big tap targets for logging weight and reps, drop sets in one tap, a rest timer with sound and vibration, and instant exercise swaps when the equipment you planned is taken.

Stats

See the trend, not just the number

A GitHub-style consistency calendar and a per-exercise volume chart turn months of logged history into something you can actually read at a glance — no spreadsheet required.
